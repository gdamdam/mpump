Why I built this

I built mpump because I wanted to make music anywhere and share it instantly. Just a link. Open it, hear the beat. Or better: join in and play together, live.

Most music tools make you choose: too simple to be useful, or too complex to be fun.
I wanted a groovebox that starts instantly, gives you a spark fast, and lets you share it, jam on it, or perform it for others.

No install, no account, no personal data. Your music stays on your device.
Free forever.
