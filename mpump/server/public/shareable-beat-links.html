Shareable Beat Links

A beat that lives in a link.

mpump is built around a simple idea: the whole groove can travel as a URL. Whether you think of it as a browser groovebox, a browser drum machine, or a web electronic instrument, the beat stays playable. You do not have to bounce audio, send screenshots, or explain how to recreate it.

Open mpump Open shared beats
The Loop
Make Start from scratch or randomize into a working groove. Drums, bass, synth, and effects are already there.
Send Share the beat as a playable link. The next person does not need an account or an install to open it.
Change They can switch sounds, mutate the pattern, push the tempo, keep the kick, or rebuild the rest.
Resend The groove keeps moving instead of freezing at the export stage.
Why it matters
  • It is closer to passing around an idea than passing around a file.
  • It keeps the beat playable, not flattened into audio too early.
