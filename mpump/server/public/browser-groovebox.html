Browser Groovebox

Make beats in the browser.
Send them on.

mpump is a browser groovebox, browser drum machine, and web electronic instrument for loop-based music on iPhone, iPad, Android, laptop, and desktop. Drums, bass, synth, and effects are already there when you open it. The difference is that the whole groove can travel as a link, not just as an audio export.

Why mpump
Fast to start No install, no account, no personal tracking. Open it on iPhone, iPad, Android, or desktop and the groove is already moving.
Made for loops Built for techno, acid, and other electronic music that works in repetition, mutation, and return.
Deep enough to keep Step editor, mixer, effects, Euclidean rhythms, saved sessions, and live play are there when you want to go further.
Not a DAW clone mpump is an instrument first. The point is to get into a loop fast and keep it alive while it moves.
What makes it different

A lot of online grooveboxes, browser drum machines, and web sequencers stop at beat-making. mpump keeps going one step further: make, send, change, resend.

  • Make a groove in the browser: drums, bass, synth, and effects are ready immediately.
  • Share the groove as a link: the other person opens the same beat, not just a recording.
  • Let it mutate: they can change the beat, re-share it, and keep the loop moving.
  • Play live too: Live Jam and Live Set let people play together without streaming audio.
